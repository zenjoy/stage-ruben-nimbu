Nimbu Privacy Policy

All things concerning our privacy policy

Welcome to Nimbu. Nimbu cares about online privacy and takes the security of your information very seriously. This policy covers the collection and usage of your personal information. Please read it carefully. If you have any questions concerning our privacy policy, send us a message at help.Nimbu.com. This Privacy Policy covers the collection and usage of your personal information whenever you visit on Nimbu.com, or any other website created with Nimbu (collectively, the "Nimbu Websites"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, "you," "your," and "user" shall refer to any user and/or visitor of any of the Nimbu Websites. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, your use of any of the Nimbu Websites, and/or any of the Products and Services offered or provided by Nimbu, including the addition of new properties, shall be governed by this Privacy Policy and the Nimbu Terms and Conditions Agreement, where applicable, whether or not you register as a user. Be advised that we may also offer or provide services from time to time that are governed by the privacy policy of the respective service providers, and you agree to be further bound by any rules that are applicable to Nimbu with respect to the service providers. This Privacy Policy may change from time to time, at our sole and absolute discretion.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE BY NIMBU.

Nimbu collects user submitted information such as name and email address to send notifications to those users relating to, without limitation, the Nimbu service, special events, contests and special offers. As is standard practice on many corporate websites, Nimbu also logs non-personally identifiable information which includes, without limitation, IP address, profile information, aggregate user data, and browser type, from users of the Nimbu Websites. This data is used to improve the Nimbu Websites services, to analyze trends, to administer the the Nimbu Websites, to track user movement around the Nimbu Websites and to gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. Nimbu will not use the information collected to market directly to that person. This non-personally-identifiable information may be shared with third-parties to provide more relevant services and advertisements to users. User IP addresses are recorded for security and monitoring purposes. Nimbu uses pixel tags (tiny graphic images) to tell us what parts of the Nimbu Websites have been visited or to measure the effectiveness of searches users perform on the Nimbu Websites. Pixel tags also enable us to send email messages in a format users can read, and they also tell us whether emails have been opened to assure that we're only sending email messages that are of interest to our users. Nimbu stores all of this information in a secure database.

USE OF COOKIES.

Like most companies, Nimbu uses cookies to store user preferences and to record session information for various purposes, including, without limitation, market research to help us determine how best to provide useful information to users and to understand which parts of the Nimbu Websites and Internet services are of most interest to them, to customize the newsletter, advertising, and Web page content based on browser type and user profile information, to help track usage to help us understand which parts of the Nimbu Websites are the most popular, where our users are going, and how much time they spend there, to make usage of the Nimbu Websites even more rewarding as well as to study the effectiveness of our communications with users and to customize each user's experience and provide greater convenience. Please rest assured, Nimbu doesn't link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on the Nimbu Websites. You may be able to configure your browser to accept or reject all or some cookies or notify you when a cookie is set. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences; however, you must enable cookies from Nimbu in order to use most functions on the site.

SHARING / DISCLOSURE.

From time to time, Nimbu or a Nimbu Partner may sponsor a promotion, sweepstakes, or contest on the Nimbu Websites. In such cases users may be asked to provide personal information including name, email address or home address, or to answer questions in order to participate. Nimbu may transfer such personal information to certain ad partners that you have explicitly requested to receive information from. It will be clear at the point of collection who is collecting the personal information and whose privacy statement will apply. Nimbu does not sell or rent your contact information to marketers. To help us provide superior service, we may share your personal information with third parties who will safeguard it in accordance with this Privacy Policy. There are also times when it may be advantageous for Nimbu to make certain personal information about you available to companies that Nimbu has a strategic relationship with or that perform work for Nimbu to provide products and services to you on our behalf. These companies may help us process information, deliver products to you, manage and enhance customer data, provide customer service, assess your interest in our products and services, or conduct customer research or satisfaction surveys. These companies are also obligated to protect your personal information in accordance with Nimbu's policies. Without such information being made available, it would be difficult for you to purchase products, have products delivered to you, receive customer service, provide us feedback to improve our products and services, or access certain services, offers, and content on the Nimbu website. Nimbu will not disclose personal information to any third-party unless in accordance with Section 10 of this Privacy Policy. Nimbu reserves the right to transfer personal information to a successor in interest that acquires rights to that information as a result of the sale of Nimbu or substantially all of its assets to that successor in interest.

LINKS TO THIRD-PARTY WEBSITES.

Nimbu contains links to third party websites. Nimbu is not responsible for the privacy policies and/or practices of or on other websites. When linking to another website, a user should read the privacy policy stated on that website. Our privacy policy only governs information collected on the Nimbu Websites.

SHARING / DISCLOSURE.

Nimbu may send email messages which use or otherwise contain a "click-through URL" that is linked to content on the Nimbu Websites. When the "click-through URL" is clicked on or otherwise activated, the party who clicked on or otherwise activated the "click-through URL" is taken through the Nimbu web server(s) before arriving at the destination page. During this process, "click-through data" is collected by Nimbu, which may include, without limitation, data that helps us determine interest in particular topics and data that helps us measure the effectiveness of the communications we send out. If you prefer not to be tracked in this manner, simply avoid clicking on or otherwise activating text or graphic links in the email.

CONCERNING CONTENT YOU ADD TO NIMBU.

Any content that you add, upload, post, publish, or otherwise disseminate through the Products and Services offered by Nimbu should be considered fully accessible by the public and not private. Such content, once published, may immediately become accessible on search engines and other sites. It is important that you are mindful of this fact.

EMAIL PREFERENCES / OPTING-OUT.

Occasionally, Nimbu may send email regarding news, promotions, surveys, updates, or offers related to Nimbu. Users who no longer wish to receive these emails may choose not to by simply opting-out. To opt-out, simply click on the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of the email. It is important to note, users cannot opt-out of email regarding legal notices or user account changes.

SECURITY.

Nimbu Accounts are secured by user-created (as applicable) passwords. Nimbu takes precautions to ensure that account information is kept private through reasonable measures such as encryption to protect private information that is stored within our database, and we restrict access to such private information to those employees who need access to perform their job functions, such as our customer service personnel and technical staff. Please note, however, that Nimbu cannot and does not guarantee the security of user account information. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user account information at any time. Users are urged to take precautions to protect their personal data by changing passwords often, using a combination of letters and numbers, and using a secure web browser. For any additional information about the security measures Nimbu uses on the Nimbu Websites, please contact us at info@nimbu.io.
