Your Own Domain Name

Use your own domain name, or purchase one through Nimbu. Nimbu lets you map your domain transparently over ours, ensuring visitors never see a Nimbu domain when viewing your site.

Anti-Spam

Nimbu is a paid service that aggressively fights spam. If you are using a free service that is a target for spammers, you potentially run the risk of being falsely grouped with other spam sites.

Custom Code
